inline

Vector of measurement positions in the circuit, i.e. the indexes where the measurements take place.

Note
If there are more consecutive measurements after step S, then their indexes will all be S, i.e. it is always assumed that the measurements taking place immediately after a gate step have the same index as the preceding gate step.
